Utilities in the CPLEX Callable Library (C API)

These routines initialize and close the CPLEX environment.

  • CPXXcloseCPLEX and CPXcloseCPLEX
    This routine frees all of the data structures associated with CPLEX.
  • CPXXopenCPLEX and CPXopenCPLEX
    The routine CPXXopenCPLEX/CPXopenCPLEX initializes a CPLEX environment.
  • CPXXsetterminate and CPXsetterminate
    This routine enables applications to terminate CPLEX gracefully.
