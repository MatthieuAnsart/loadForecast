What is a goal?

Defines a goal in CPLEX.

Goals allow you to take control of the branch & cut search procedure used by IBM ILOG CPLEX to solve MIP problems. To help you understand how to use goals with CPLEX, these sections review how this procedure works.

Note:

Goals are implemented by IloCplex::Goal, not IloGoal as in other IBM ILOG products.
