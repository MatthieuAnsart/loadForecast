Accessing basis information

Describes methods in the C++ API to retrieve basis information.

When you solve LPs or QPs with either the simplex algorithm or the barrier optimizer with crossover enabled, basis information is available as well. You can consult basis information by means of the method getStatuses which returns basis status information for variables and constraints.

Such information is encoded by the nested enumeration IloCplex::BasisStatus.
