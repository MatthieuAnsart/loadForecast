Changes in licensing

Announces a change in licensing of CPLEX for many users.

ILM, the ILOG license manager, has been removed from most editions of CPLEX available from IBM. Exceptionally, ILM is still present in the edition of CPLEX available from the IBM Academic Initiative.

Sub-capacity licensing is available from IBM Passport Advantage. For information about purchasing sub-capacity licensing from IBM Passport Advantage, contact your IBM representative.
