Sifting and concurrent optimization of LP

Explains changes introduced by CPLEX V12.6.1 that apply to users of concurrent optimization for LP models.

In CPLEX V12.6.1, concurrent optimization of a linear program (LP) considers the aspect ratio of the model that it solves. If the aspect ratio of the model is large, and if more than 10 (ten) threads are available to CPLEX, then concurrent optimization of an LP also includes sifting.

For more information about concurrent optimization, see the CPLEX User's Manual, especially the topic Concurrent optimizer in parallel.

For more information about sifting, see the CPLEX User's Manual, especially the topic Sifting optimizer.
