Deprecated: _stdcall

Deprecated feature for Windows users

In previous releases of CPLEX, cplex.DLL made all function call symbols available with undecorated names (that is, without the suffix @N) even though the functions were, in fact, _stdcall functions.

This convention has been deprecated in CPLEX V12.5.1. The convention will be removed from a future release of the product.

In future releases of CPLEX, functions will be exported from cplex.DLL only once: either with the suffix @N if they are _stdcall functions or without that suffix if they are not _stdcall functions.
