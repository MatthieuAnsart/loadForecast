Compile-time warnings about deprecated items

Describes a new compile-time feature: warnings about use of deprecated methods, functions, routines.

Deprecated elements (such as methods, classes, routines, macros, and so forth) in CPLEX V12.3 are now tagged to produce warnings at compile-time. Depending on the compiler and warning-level that you use, you can see warnings issued by the compiler each time you use a deprecated item of CPLEX V12.3. This warning allows you to prepare for the removal of the deprecated item, if your compiler and choice of warning level supports such warnings. The CPLEX reference documentation of such deprecated elements suggests suitable replacements for your application.
