MATLAB new features

Lists new functions in the CPLEX connector for MATLAB.

These new functions of the toolbox are available to users of the CPLEX connector for The MathWorks MATLAB:

  • cplexlsqbilp to solve constrained binary integer least squares problems;

  • cplexlsqnonnegqcp to solve nonnegative quadratically constrained least squares problems;

  • cplexlsqqcp to solve quadratically constrained least squares problems.

For examples of those new functions in use, see Examples of the CPLEX for MATLAB Toolbox.

The CPLEX connector for The MathWorks MATLAB also now offers the same features to analyze the quality of a solution as other components of CPLEX. See the new documentation of the property Cplex.Solution for more information about this feature in CPLEX for MATLAB, and see also the topic Interpreting solution quality in the CPLEX User’s Manual for more general information about analyzing the quality of a solution.
