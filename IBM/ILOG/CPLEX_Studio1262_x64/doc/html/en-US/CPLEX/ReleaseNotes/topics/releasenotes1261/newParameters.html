New CPLEX parameters

Announces new parameters of CPLEX V12.6.1.

These notes announce new parameters available in CPLEX V12.6.1.

  • QToLin: linearization switch for QP, MIQP
    Announces a new parameter of CPLEX V12.6.1 to turn on or off linearization of the quadratic terms in the objective function of a QP or MIQP model.
  • Locally valid implied bound cuts
    Announces a new parameter of CPLEX V12.6.1 to switch on or off generation of locally valid implied bound cuts in a MIP.
  • Conflict refiner algorithm
    Announces a new parameter of CPLEX V12.6.1 to choose the algorithm CPLEX uses in the conflict refiner.
