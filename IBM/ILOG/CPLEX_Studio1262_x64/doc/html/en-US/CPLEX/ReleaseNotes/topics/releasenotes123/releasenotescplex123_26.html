New features for users of Excel

Describes new features in CPLEX V12.3 for users of the CPLEX connector for Excel

64-bit Excel connector

In CPLEX V12.3, the CPLEX connector for Excel is compatible with versions of Excel that also support 64-bits, such as Excel in Microsoft Office 2010. For an introduction to the CPLEX connector for Excel, see the online user’s manual available in an Excel session.
