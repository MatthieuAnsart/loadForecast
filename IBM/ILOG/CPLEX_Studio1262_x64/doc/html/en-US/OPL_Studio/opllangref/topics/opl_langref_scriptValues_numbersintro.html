Introduction

Provides an overview of how numbers are expressed in OPL.

Numbers can be expressed in decimal (base 10), hexadecimal (base 16), or octal (base 8.) There are also special numbers.

Note:

For C/C++ programmers: Numbers have the same syntax as C and C++ integers and doubles. They are internally represented as 64-bit double-precision floating-point numbers.
