OPL model library

The OPL model library is a collection of OPL models that describe known problems in the academic and industrial fields.

  • Where to find the industry examples
    Describes where to find the academic and industry examples in the CPLEX Studio distribution.
  • Finance
    Describes examples of libraries that involve financial problems such as bid selection, capital budgeting, and portfolio optimization.
  • Manufacturing
    Describes examples of libraries that involve manufacturing problems such as blending, planning, and steel production.
  • Mining
    Describes examples of libraries that involve mining problems such as mine planning and opencast mining.
  • Supply chain
    Provides examples of libraries that involve supply chain problems such as decentralization, multistage supply chain, outsourcing, and product deployment.
  • Telecommunications
    Provides an example of a model that is used to construct a network route.
  • Time tabling
    Describes examples of libraries that involve timetabling problems such as basketball game scheduling, manpower planning, and staff assignment.
  • Transportation
    Provides examples of libraries that involve transportation problems such as airline management, flight pairing, fleet assignment, and network flows.
  • Mathematical games
    Describes a model that illustrates a didactic mathematical game.
