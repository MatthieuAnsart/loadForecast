Useful concepts and terms

Describes terms and concepts that are useful in the understanding of the CPLEX® Studio IDE.

  • Resources
    Resources are the projects, folders, and files that exist in the CPLEX Studio IDE.
  • Workspace
    The workspace is the working directory in which you store your resources and work with them.
  • Views
    The various panes or windows within the CPLEX Studio IDE are referred to as Views.
