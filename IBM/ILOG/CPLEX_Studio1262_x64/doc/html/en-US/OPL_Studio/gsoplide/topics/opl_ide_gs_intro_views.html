Views

The various panes or windows within the CPLEX® Studio IDE are referred to as Views.

Views can be editors or navigators, or provide alternative ways to visualize your projects and work with them. For example, the OPL Projects Navigator view displays the resources in your OPL projects, and allows you to open them in editor views.

Views may also have their own menus, and some views have their own toolbars.
