View the LP file in the IDE

After exporting a model to an LP file, you can use the LP viewer to find references to a variable.

Click on a variable in the .lp file. All occurrences of the variable are highlighted in the LP viewer. In the lower pane, the lines containing the variable are displayed.

In the example above, the variable Addcap("Steel")(4) is selected in the LP viewer. In the pane below the viewer, the line numbers of all 6 occurrences are displayed.
