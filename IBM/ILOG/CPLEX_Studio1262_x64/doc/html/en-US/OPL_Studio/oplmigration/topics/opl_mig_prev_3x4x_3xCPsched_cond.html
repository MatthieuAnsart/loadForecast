Conditions for migration

General information to be aware of before you begin your migration.

All the modeling features of OPL 3.7 are subsumed by a feature of the new scheduling language except for:

  • discrete energy resources

If your OPL 3.7 model contains this feature, then it may be difficult to migrate. Depending on the situation, you can use alternative models:

  • In case the size of the buckets of the discrete energy resource is small compared with the typical duration of activities, you can consider formulating the discrete energy resource as a discrete resource (see the Cumulative resources section below)

  • In case the number of buckets is reasonably small, you can bucketize the time by defining an integer variable for each time bucket that represents the total energy used by activities in the bucket.
