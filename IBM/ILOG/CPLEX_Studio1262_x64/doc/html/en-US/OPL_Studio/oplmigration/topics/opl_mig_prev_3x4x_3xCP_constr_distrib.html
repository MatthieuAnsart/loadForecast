distribute

Gives the syntax and an example of distribute for OPL 3.x and OPL 6.x.

In OPL 3.x

distribute(cardi,  value, base)

In OPL 6.x

count 

forall(i in RangeOfValue) {
     count(base, value[i]) == cardi[i];
