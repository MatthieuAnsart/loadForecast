Other

Describes some additional changes in OPL 3.x syntax necessary for compatibility with OPL 4 and later.

  1. Replace \ with \\ to escape backslashes and ends of line. See Literal strings for details.

  2. Replace the slash as division operator / by div when the divisor is an integer and if you want an integer division. See Integer division.
