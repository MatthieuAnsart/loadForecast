Licensing - trial version

Describes the licensing for the OPL-CPLEX Analyst Studio online trial version.

OPL-CPLEX Analyst Studio has an online trial version. It is the regular OPL installer, but no license keys are issued.

If OPL finds no license keys, it restricts its usage as follows:

  • Only the IDE is available.

  • CPLEX is available, but limited to only 500 constraints and 500 variables.

  • CP Optimizer is available, but the search space size, before propagation, is restricted to the equivalent of 1000 binary (0-1) variables.
