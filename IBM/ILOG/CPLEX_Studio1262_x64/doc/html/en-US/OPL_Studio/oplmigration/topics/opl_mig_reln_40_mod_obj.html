Objectives

Describes syntax changes in the objectives.

Objectives must be terminated with a semi-colon ; but the final semi-colon, after the subject to block, is no longer required.

Parent topic: The modeling language

In OPL 3

You wrote:

maximize
    sum (p in Products) profit[p] * production[p]
subject to { ... };

In OPL 4

You write:

maximize
    sum (p in Products) profit[p] * production[p];
subject to { ... }
