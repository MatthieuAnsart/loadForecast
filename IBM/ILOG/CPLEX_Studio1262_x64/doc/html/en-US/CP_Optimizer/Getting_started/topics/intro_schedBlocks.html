Building blocks of scheduling models

The basic scheduling building blocks available in CP Optimizer include interval variables and specialized constraints.

  • Overview of scheduling
    The basic scheduling building blocks available in CP Optimizer represent time intervals and relationships amongst those intervals.
  • Modeling time intervals in scheduling problems using interval variables
    Time intervals are the definitions of activities, operations or tasks to be completed. Time intervals are represented in CP Optimizer by interval decision variables.
  • Modeling constraints on interval variables
    Scheduling constraints, including precedence, no overlap, and other constraints, are available in CP Optimizer.
